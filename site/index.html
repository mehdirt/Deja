It works the way memory should — quietly

No buttons to press, no copy-paste, no folders to maintain. You keep using your AI tools exactly as you do today; Deja does the remembering.

Captures automatically

Every prompt you send is saved the moment you hit Enter — across five AI tools, with no effort from you.

Resurfaces in context

Start typing and Deja floats a gentle reminder when you've asked something similar before. Click to copy — it never types for you.

Finds anything fast

Fuzzy search, tags, pins, and favorites. Sort by newest, most useful, most-used, or longest-unseen. Built for a library of hundreds.

Yours to keep

Export and import JSON, export Markdown, blocklist sites you don't want captured, or wipe everything in one click.

Private by design — not by promise

Deja can't leak what it never sends. Everything stays in your browser.

  • No network calls, no telemetry, no analytics. Nothing you type ever leaves your machine.
  • No accounts, no cloud, no third parties. Your library lives on your machine, in your browser (local IndexedDB).
  • Only your prompt text is saved — never the AI's replies.
  • Credential-aware. Password, one-time-code, and payment fields are never read.
